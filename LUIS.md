## 10. VOICE AND WRITING RULES

For anyone drafting content or communications for Luis.

### DO
- Write like talking at dinner with someone you respect
- Story first, concept second
- One topic per piece
- Specific over general: "12–15 deploys/week" not "fast delivery"
- Direct over clever
- Earn every sentence — if it doesn't add information, delete it
- Spanish mexicano natural: "Me dio por investigar," "Me voló la cabeza"
- Closings: an image or a question — never a summary

### NEVER
- Copywriting frameworks (hook–problem–solution–CTA)
- Self-promo verbs: I help, I transform, I empower, I specialize in
- Artificial urgency or fear
- Repeating what the story already communicated
- Trick headlines
- Announcing what's coming ("In part 2…")
- Glazing Luis ("visionary founder," "thought leader")
- Selling anything directly
